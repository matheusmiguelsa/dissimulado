170. A Security Engineer creates an Amazon S3 bucket policy that denies access to all users. A few
days later, the Security Engineer adds an additional statement to the bucket policy to allow read-only
access to one other employee. Even after updating the policy, the employee still receives an access
denied message.

What is the likely cause of this access denial?

A. The ACL in the bucket needs to be updated

B. The IAM policy does not allow the user to access the bucket

C. It takes a few minutes for a bucket policy to take effect

D. The allow permission is being overridden by the deny Most Voted

Answer: D
