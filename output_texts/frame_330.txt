163. A company needs a forensic-logging solution for hundreds of applications running in Docker on
Amazon EC2. The solution must perform real-time analytics on the logs, must support the replay of
messages, and must persist the logs.

Which AWS services should be used to meet these requirements? (Choose two.)
A. Amazon Athena

B. Amazon Kinesis

C. Amazon SQS

D. Amazon Elasticsearch

E. Amazon EMR

Answer: B, D
