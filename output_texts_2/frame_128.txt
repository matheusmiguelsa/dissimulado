289) A developer signed in to a new account within an [AM Organization organizational unit (OU) containing multiple
accounts. Access to the Amazon S3 service is restricted with the following SCP.

How can the security engineer provide the developer with Amazon $3 access without affecting other account?

A.Move the SCP to the root OU of organization to remove the restriction to access Amazon S3.
B.Add an IAM policy for the developer, which grants S3 access.

C.Create a new OU without applying the SCP restricting S3 access. Move the developer account to this new
OU.

D.Add an allow list for the developer account for the $3 service.

Answer: C
