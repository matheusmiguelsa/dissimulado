419) A healthcare company has multiple AWS accounts in an organization in AWS Organizations. The company uses
Amazon S3 buckets to store sensitive information of patients. The company needs to restrict users from deleting any $3
bucket across the organization.

What is the MOST scalable solution that meets these requirements?
A. Permissions boundaries in AWS Identity and Access Management (1AM)
B. S3 bucket policies

C. Tag policies
D. SCPs

Correct Answer: D
