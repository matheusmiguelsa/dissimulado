219. A company is setting up products to deploy in AWS Service Catalog. Management is concerned
that when users launch products, elevated IAM privileges will be required to create resources.

How should the company mitigate this concern?

A. Add a template constraint to each product in the portfolio.

B. Add a launch constraint to each product in the portfolio.

C. Define resource update constraints for each product in the portfolio.

D. Update the AWS CloudFormation template backing the product to include a service role
configuration.

Answer: B
